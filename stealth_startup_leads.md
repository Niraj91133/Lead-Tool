### 🛠️ The "Founder Email" Cheat Sheet:
Startups in YC standardly use these patterns:
1. `[first]@company.com` (Example: `devi@cardinal.ai`)
2. `[first].[last]@company.com` (Example: `devi.jha@cardinal.ai`)
3. `founders@company.com` (Universal for the founding team)
