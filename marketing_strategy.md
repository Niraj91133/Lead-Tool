# US Marketing Strategy & Cold Email Templates

This document outlines the strategy for selling high-converting landing pages to the US market.

## 1. Key Strategy Principles
* **Sell Outcomes, Not Features:** Focus on ROI, conversion rates, and reduced ad spend rather than just "pretty design."
* **Consultative Approach:** Position yourself as a partner/specialist who identifies and fixes "leaks" in their sales funnel.
* **Personalization is Mandatory:** US high-ticket clients reject generic templates. Mention their company name and a specific issue on their site.
* **Low-Friction Entry:** Offer a free 2-minute video audit or a specific suggestion before asking for a call.

## 2. Legal & Technical Compliance (CAN-SPAM Act)
* **Unsubscribe Link:** Always include a way for people to opt-out.
* **Physical Address:** Include a physical or mailing address in the footer.
* **Clear Subject Lines:** No deceptive subjects (e.g., "Re:" in the first touch).
* **Technical Setup:** Use a secondary domain, warm it up, and ensure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC are set.
